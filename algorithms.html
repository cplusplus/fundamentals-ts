Shuffle

template<class RandomAccessIterator> void shuffle(RandomAccessIterator first, RandomAccessIterator last); Permutes the elements in the range [first,last) such that each possible permutation of those elements has equal probability of appearance. RandomAccessIterator shall satisfy the requirements of ValueSwappable (). Exactly (last - first) - 1 swaps.
